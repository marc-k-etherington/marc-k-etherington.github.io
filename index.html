Welcome to The Etherington Lab!

We are a research group based at Northumbria University (Newcastle upon Tyne) studying the photophysics of organic light-emitting materials, with particular expertise in thermally activated delayed fluorescence (TADF) and through-space charge-transfer processes. Using advanced spectroscopy and molecular design, we explore fundamental excited-state mechanisms to develop efficient emitters for applications ranging from flexible displays and bioimaging to chemical sensing and photonics.
