Marc and Aidan travelled to Technische Universität Dresden for a British Council-funded Springboard Meeting, bringing the full consortium together to discuss the next stages of our collaboration and future funding ideas. It was fantastic to have everyone in one place: the teams of Sebastian Reineke, Sebastian Schellhammer and Christina Dornack, with additional support from Paul-Anton Will (PRUUVE GmbH) and Simon FitzGerald from HORIBA UK LIMITED joining us online.

Beyond the science, we made the most of the trip by connecting over coffee, enjoying some rare March sunshine over lunch, and being inspired by excellent research during the lab tours.

Watch this space for what comes next! 👀
