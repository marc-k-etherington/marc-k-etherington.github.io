Marc visited the groups of Jenny Clark and Paloma dos Santos to discuss a potential new collaboration. It was a brilliantly sunny day and after a presentation and then discussion in the morning Marc was given a tour of the joint facilities that the two groups share. They went to Rudy's pizzeria for lunch and then Marc admired the architure of the Mappin Building while catching up on some emails. Keep an eye out for the results of this new collaboration!
